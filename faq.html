Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you unleash the fury.

Is this actually safe?

Absolutely. Your safety is our top priority. Before you enter the room, you will receive a mandatory safety briefing and watch a demonstration video. You will be equipped with a full set of professional-grade safety gear: a protective coverall ("bunny suit"), heavy-duty boots, a welding apron, impact-resistant welding gloves, and a full-face motorcycle helmet. All sessions are monitored by our staff to ensure rules are followed.

What should I wear?

We require all participants to wear closed-toe shoes (boots are ideal, sneakers are acceptable, but no sandals or heels). We also recommend comfortable clothing that you can easily move around in, as our protective gear will be worn over your clothes.

What am I allowed to break?

You'll be breaking items provided by us, which are included in your package. This typically includes a mix of glassware, plates, printers, monitors, lamps, and other electronics. Prohibited items include anything containing hazardous materials like light bulbs, batteries, or pressurized containers. You are not permitted to break the structure of the room itself (walls, floor, door, camera).

Can I bring my own stuff to smash?

For safety and environmental reasons, we do not currently allow outside items. Our inventory is carefully sourced to ensure it is safe to break and can be disposed of responsibly. We promise we have plenty of satisfyingly smashable items for you!

Can I get a video of my session?

Yes! Each room is equipped with a high-quality camera. You can add a video recording of your session to any package for a small additional fee. It's the perfect memento of your destructive therapy.

What is the age limit? Can I participate if I'm pregnant?

All participants must be 18 years of age or older and provide a valid ID upon arrival. For safety reasons, pregnant individuals are not permitted to participate in rage room activities.

Do I have to clean up the mess?

Absolutely not. Your only job is to have a cathartic experience. Once your time is up, you can de-gear and relax in our cool-down lounge. We handle the entire cleanup and responsible disposal of all materials.
